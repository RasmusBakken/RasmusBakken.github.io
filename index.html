    • Diagonalisation – The Road to Infinities, Truth and Gödel (given with Mahin Hossain) – Philosophy Faculty, Oxford [Michaelmas 2025]

      This was a series of four lectures on diagonalisation in mathematics and the various fruits of diagonal arguments. We explored Cantor's diagonal argument, Tarski's Undefinability of Truth, and Gödel's Incompleteness Theorems, from both a mathematical and a philosophical point of view.
